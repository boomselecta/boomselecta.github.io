Digital superfood for red-carpet PR results.

 

 

 

 

 

PR Booster is a free tool that helps PR associates quickly identify the right publications to pitch; the publications that will allow your paid search (SEM) team to include snippets of your best PR placements directly within Google text ad "Review Extensions" at the top of the Google search results page.

PR Booster was created by the SEM experts at Good Apple Digital, a boutique media planning and buying agency located in New York City's Union Square.

Review Extensions place your best PR at the top of the Google Search Engine Results Page (SERP), allowing you to:

  • Rejuvenate your best PR placements
  • Connect your articles to new audiences
  • Focus on the right publications
  • Achieve your campaign goals faster
  • Save time at work, so you can get to Yoga
