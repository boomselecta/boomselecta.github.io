PR Booster was created by the SEM experts at Good Apple Digital, a boutique media planning and buying agency located in New York City's Union Square. The mission of PR Booster is to help public relations associates quickly identify publications that will allow snippets of their articles to be included in Google AdWords text ads in the form of "Review Extensions". Review Extensions appear within branded SEM text ads, usually at the top of the Google Search Engine Results Page (SERP) every time a user searches for your clients' brand names.
